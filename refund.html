Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Digital Product Policy

Since Capital Mindset (CMS Pro) is a non-tangible, irrevocable digital software/indicator, we do not issue refunds once the order is processed and access to the script is granted.

2. Cancellations

You may cancel your monthly or quarterly subscription at any time to prevent future billing. However, no prorated refunds will be issued for the remaining days of your active billing cycle.

3. Exceptional Circumstances

If you face a technical issue where the software completely fails to deploy on your TradingView account, please contact our support team on WhatsApp or Telegram within 48 hours for technical assistance.
